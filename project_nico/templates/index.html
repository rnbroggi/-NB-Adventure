Have you ever wanted to write your stories as if you were any of your characters?

Lucky you! NB-Adventure site is your character personal journal!

If you are the kind of person that loves writing little fantasy stories, this shire may help you inspire your adventures. You can create fantasy characters named whatever you want and write their backstory as you please. In NB-Adventure your characters have their own journal where you can write as if you were just one of them

First you need to create an account. After you log in you should create one or many characters. They don't need to be only humans! Why not elves, dwarves or even orcs? You can even choose a class that may fit your stories, such as mages or pirates. Once you created your fantasy character you can open your journal and write posts as if it were handwritten old stories. Well, that was how personal blogs worked centuries ago, right?

If you don't feel too inspired, NB-Adventure also offers you a (very) simple turn-based RPG game. Where you can fight and slay different creatures during your journey. So when you are resting after some tough fights you can write down your feats of strength.

Well, what are you waiting for fellow writer? {# Link changes if authenticated #} Go ahead and start your adventure!
